📊 How is your rate calculated? ▾
CPM — Cost Per Mille (per 1,000 views)
What brands pay for every 1,000 times your content is seen. Finance brands pay R634/1000 views; Lifestyle pays R218/1000 views.
CPE — Cost Per Engagement
What a brand pays per like, comment, or share. SA creators average 3.39% ER (global: 1.49%) — this is your competitive edge.
Why we use MAX(CPM, CPE)
Your rate is the higher of the two. This protects your maximum value whether a brand values reach or engagement.
CPM Rate = (niche_cpm × tier_mult × platform_mult × content_mult) / 1000 × avg_views
CPE Rate = cpe_zar × platform_cpe_mult × (avg_likes + avg_comments + avg_shares)
Final = MAX(CPM Rate, CPE Rate) × premium_multiplier
Range = Final × 0.85 → Final × 1.15

SA ER benchmark: 3.39% average (global: 1.49%). Most SA creators fall in the "High" or "Premium" CPE tier.
