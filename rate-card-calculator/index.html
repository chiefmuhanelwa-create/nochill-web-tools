How We Calculate Your Rates

Industry-standard pricing methods calibrated for the South African influencer market.

👁️
CPM Method
Cost Per Mille — what brands pay per 1,000 views. Finance brands pay R634/1000 views; Lifestyle pays R218. Best for awareness campaigns.
✓ Reach-based pricing
🎯
CPE Method
Cost Per Engagement — based on likes, comments, shares. SA creators average 3.39% ER (global: 1.49%). Your engagement is your edge.
✓ Engagement-based pricing
📐 The Formula
CPM Rate = (niche_cpm × tier_mult × platform_mult × content_mult) / 1000 × total_views CPE Rate = cpe_zar × platform_cpe_mult × total_interactions Base Rate = MAX(CPM Rate, CPE Rate) × premium_multiplier Total = Base Rate × campaign_mult + production_cost Range = Total × 0.85 → Total × 1.15

Your rate is always the higher of CPM vs CPE — protecting your maximum value. SA ER benchmark: 3.39% avg (global: 1.49%).

CPM benchmarks reflect 2024–2025 South African market rates. Creators in other African markets (Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana) should treat these as upper-range references and adjust based on local brand budgets.
